I am an Assistant Professor at the University of Groningen with several years of postdoctoral research experience at Delft University of Technology and the University of California, Irvine. I have also worked as a visiting researcher and research intern at Microsoft Research, Redmond. I earned my Ph.D. from IIIT-Delhi, India.

My expertise lies in data analytics and artificial intelligence for software engineering, with a focus on analytics for empirical software engineering. My research addresses challenges impacting software companies, open-source communities, and society.

I am an active member of Netherlands' (IPN EDI) and Europe-wide (Informatics Europe) ICT initiatives for Equity, Diversity, and Inclusion.
