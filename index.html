I am an Assistant Professor at the University of Groningen. I have several years of postdoctoral research experience at the Delft University of Technology and the University of California-Irvine. I was a visiting researcher and research intern at Microsoft Research, Redmond. I completed my Ph.D. at IIIT-Delhi, India.

I specialize in data analytics and artificial intelligence for software engineering, focusing on analytics for empirical software engineering. My research projects solve problems relevant to software companies, open-source, and society.

I am an active member of Netherlands' (IPN EDI) and Europe-wide (Informatics Europe) ICT initiatives for Equity, Diversity, and Inclusion.
